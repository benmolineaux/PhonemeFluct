Note that red markers represent languages that are widely spoken and better documented. As such, they are treated as reference points and we do not plan to create extensive corpus resources for them.

The markers in orange belong to languages outside of scope for the current project, nevertheless, these are still within the broader Southern Cone area of interest and initial data will be gathered for triangulation with the focal languages.
